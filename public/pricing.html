Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start free?

Yes! The free plan lets you create up to 3 APIs with 10K requests/month. No credit card required.

How does revenue sharing work?

When someone pays to use your API, the platform takes a small fee (5-20% depending on your plan). The rest goes directly to your Stripe account.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change plans at any time. Upgrades are instant. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens if I exceed my request limit?

We'll notify you at 80% usage. If you exceed the limit, APIs continue working but new requests queue until the next billing cycle, or you can upgrade instantly.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.
